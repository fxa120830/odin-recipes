This is how you cook a medium rare steak.



This is what is needed to cook a steak.
  • Steak
  • Grill
  • Butter
  • Seasoning and Salt

these are the steps to cook a steak
  1. Throw seasoning and butter onto steak. Let it cool for 12 hours in the fridge.
  2. Throw onto the grill, and let it cook for about 10 minutes.
  3. You will know it is medium rare when it is firm but has a little bounce to it.
  4. Enjoy
