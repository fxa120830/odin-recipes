This is my recipe on how to make tacos.

I love making tacos de carne asada, they are the best.


This is what you will need
  • Skirt Steak
  • Tortillas
  • Limes

These are the steps to make tacos.
  1. Cook the skirt steak.
  2. Cut the skirt steak into strips.
  3. Cook the tortillas.
  4. Place the skirt steak strips on the tortillas. Throw some lime for extra flavor.
  5. Enjoy.
