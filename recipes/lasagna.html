This is the recipe to create a lasagna dish.



This what you will need to make a delicious lasagna dish that my grandma would make

Ingredient List
  • Pasta
  • Tomato
  • Water
  • Pot
  • Salt
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Pan

Step by step process
  1. First pour water in the pot and set it to boil.
  2. Throw pasta in the pot and then add salt.
  3. Once the pasta is nice and soft, strain the water out and set it to the side.
  4. Cook the ground beef. Make sure to add your favorite seasoning.
  5. Start by layering the pasta then sauce then cheese then ground beef in this order in layers until the pan is filled to the top
  6. Throw in the oven at 400 degrees until golden brown.
  7. Let it cool for 5 minutes. Enjoy.
